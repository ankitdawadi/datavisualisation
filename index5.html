US Education Mapping

Percentage of adults with bachelor certificate Source: USDA Economic Research Service

In this data visualisation, we visualise chloropleth map. The map gives the information about the completion of bachelor by the US adult accoring to county data. To draw the county of US we use topo json, where the longitude and latitude of different counties were used to create a path eventually drawing the counties. Later the counties information was mapped with the education data to form the final diagram.
